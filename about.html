SMU Libraries Spaces 0.0.1
Spaces introduces SMU Libraries' two physical libraries through photos and quick facts.

SMU Libraries is the heartbeat of the University, a centre where researchers and learners converge to share and acquire academic and professional knowledge.

Together, Li Ka Shing Library and Kwa Geok Choo Law Library offer more than 11,000m2 of learning spaces to the SMU community.
